Simple Risk View

Crypto investing can rise quickly and fall quickly. Risk control is part of the strategy, not an afterthought.

  • No single asset should dominate your plan.
  • Use pre-set invalidation points before entry.
  • Keep capital in stages instead of one large move.
  • Review news events that can shift sentiment fast.

How we reduce risk: structure, checkpoints, and clear stop logic.
